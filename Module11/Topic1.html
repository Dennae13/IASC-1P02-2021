Croissants

This type of food is usually eaten for breakfast. It is a cheap, flaky, and buttery pastry that takes time and skill to prepare (Picard, 2020).

Escargots

This type of food is said to be a national symbol. Escargots are snails and are popularly cooked with the Burgundy recipe (Picard, 2020). The Burgundy recipe is when the snails are cooked in court bouillon and the shell (with the snail still inside) is topped with a sauce that contains butter, parsley, and chopped garlic (Burgundy Snails - Escargots de Bourgogne, 2016).

Macarons

These French sweets are known for their airy and delicate shells that are made from almond flour (Picard, 2020). They also have a rich creamy filling that can have a variety of flavors like chocolate, vanilla, pistachio, and many more (Picard, 2020)!

Jambon-beurre

This type of street food is a simple lunch snack. It has three ingredients, Parisian ham, butter, and a crispy baguette (Picard, 2020).

Steak tartare

The ingredients of this bistro classic are raw ground beef seasoned with capers, black pepper, onion and is usually served with a raw egg yolk on top (Picard, 2020).

Cheese

France has more than 300 varieties of cheeses and is a very popular treat that is eaten all the time (Picard, 2020).

Onion Soup

This Parisian comfort food is said to be invented by Louis XV nearly three centuries ago, it contains beef broth and caramelized onion served with croutons and a slice of Gruyère cheese on top (Picard, 2020).
